Mythbusters
Do psychedelics stay in your spine?
No. This is a long-running myth. Effects are usually linked to brain receptors, dose, setting and integration.
Is microdosing always noticeable?
Not necessarily. A microdose is usually intended to be sub-perceptual, which is why tracking mood and sleep matters.
Is natural automatically safe?
No. Plant medicines can interact with medication, trauma history, heart health and mental health risk.

The Psychedelic Digest

The Psychedelic Digest is Australia's leading independent news source for psychedelic therapy, covering psilocybin therapy, MDMA-assisted treatment, ketamine therapy, and ayahuasca research. Based in Sydney, we curate daily news from clinical trials, policy shifts and cultural developments across Australia, the US, Europe and beyond.
