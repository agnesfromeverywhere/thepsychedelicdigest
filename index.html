Mythbusters
Do psychedelics stay in your spine?
No. This is a persistent myth. Effects link to brain receptors, dose, setting and integration.
Is microdosing always noticeable?
Not necessarily. A microdose is sub-perceptual by design — tracking mood and sleep is key.
Is natural automatically safe?
No. Plant medicines interact with medications, trauma history and cardiac health.

The Psychedelic Digest

The Psychedelic Digest is Australia's leading independent news source for psychedelic therapy, covering psilocybin therapy, MDMA-assisted treatment, ketamine therapy, and ayahuasca research. Based in Sydney, we curate daily news from clinical trials, policy shifts and cultural developments across Australia, the US, Europe and beyond. Whether you are a clinician seeking psychedelic therapy resources, a patient exploring treatment options in Australia, or simply curious about the future of consciousness medicine — this is your daily digest.
