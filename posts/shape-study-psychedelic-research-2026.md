**The SHAPE study is a Swiss-run research project paying EUR 50 to people who have already decided to use a psychedelic, in exchange for completing five online surveys before and after their experience.** It is run by the Lake Lucerne Institute and the University of Fribourg, and it exists to build harm-reduction science from real-world psychedelic use rather than clinical trials.

![Lake Lucerne Institute SHAPE study](/images/trial-lake-lucerne.png)

## What is the SHAPE study?

SHAPE stands for **Studying Human Attributes After a Psychedelic Experience**. It is a longitudinal, online research study tracking how real-world psychedelic experiences affect human attributes over several weeks. Unlike clinical trials, SHAPE does not administer substances. It only surveys people who have independently already decided to use one.

## Who can join the SHAPE study?

You qualify if you have **already decided** to use one of the following substances:

- LSD
- Psilocybin
- Ayahuasca
- DMT
- MDMA

The researchers are explicit: they do not provide, source, or promote any substance. Participation is limited to people who have made that decision on their own, independent of the study.

## What does participation involve?

Participants complete **five online surveys**, each taking 20 to 30 minutes, timed before and after their planned experience. The study runs longitudinally across several weeks to measure change over time, not just a single before-and-after snapshot.

## How much does it pay?

Participants receive **EUR 50 compensation** upon completing the full survey series.

## Is the data private?

Yes. All data is **stripped of personal details and stored securely in Switzerland**, a jurisdiction with strong data protection law. The study is registered under **Ethical Approval ID BASEC-ID 2025-02370**.

## FAQ

**Does the SHAPE study provide or supply psychedelics?**
No. The study only surveys people who have already independently decided to use a substance. It does not provide, source, or promote drug use in any form.

**How long does the SHAPE study take to complete?**
Five surveys of 20 to 30 minutes each, spread across several weeks before and after your planned experience.

**Who is running the SHAPE study?**
The Lake Lucerne Institute and the University of Fribourg in Switzerland, under Ethical Approval ID BASEC-ID 2025-02370.

**What substances qualify for the SHAPE study?**
LSD, psilocybin, ayahuasca, DMT, or MDMA, provided you have already decided to use one independently of the study.
